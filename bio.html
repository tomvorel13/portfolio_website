About Me

Hi there! My name is Tom, I am 24 years old and I live near Brno, Czech Republic (Yep, I still refuse to call it Czechia). I first discovered coding back in high school, but I never thought I was quite smart enough to actually create something with code. However, at the age of 22 I decided to give it another shot and it was the best decision I have ever made. I started creating simple websites using HTML and CSS and the idea that something, that was written with words and numbers would turn into complete visual experience was just mind-boggling. Later on I also started learning more about JavaScript and responsive design and completely fell in love with it.

Apart from code, I love sports, hiking and watching documentaries. I am also a big UFC fan and a life-long Arsenal FC supporter.
