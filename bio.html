About Me

I discovered my passion for coding about a year ago and from the moment I saw my first ‘Hello World’ on my screen, I was completely addicted. After learning HTML and CSS I quickly dove into Javascript where the real fun started.

At this particular time of my young developer career, I am focusing on developing single page applications in ReactJS (I also played around in Angular a little bit) and I am trying to improve my skillset every day.

Apart from coding I love sports, watching documentaries and going on hikes.
